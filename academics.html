Academics at Jouline School

Jouline School offers a well-structured and challenging academic curriculum that ensures students are equipped with the knowledge and skills needed for future success. Our academic programs cover a broad spectrum of subjects, including Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, and Computer Studies. Additionally, we offer elective courses such as Business Studies, French, Music, and Art & Design to cater to diverse student interests.

In addition to classroom learning, we provide students with opportunities to participate in academic activities and research initiatives. We host science fairs, mathematics competitions, and literary workshops**, encouraging students to apply their knowledge beyond textbooks. Our research programs help students develop analytical and problem-solving skills through hands-on projects, guided by expert faculty members.
