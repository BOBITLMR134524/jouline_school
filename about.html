About Jouline School

Jouline School is located in the heart of Cherryhill Street, New Jersey, providing a serene and conducive environment for learning. Our school is easily accessible, surrounded by lush greenery, modern infrastructure, and a safe neighborhood that fosters academic and personal growth.

We offer a diverse curriculum that ensures our students receive a comprehensive and well-rounded education. Our core subjects include Mathematics, English, Science, Social Studies, and Computer Science. Additionally, we provide elective courses such as French, Business Studies, Music, and Environmental Studies to cater to our students' varied interests and career aspirations.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We believe in honesty and accountability.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in education.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technology.
  • Respect: We foster a culture of inclusivity and kindness.
  • Leadership: We empower students to become future leaders.

Our Motto: "Knowledge, Growth, and Success"
