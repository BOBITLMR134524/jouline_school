Extracurricular Activities

Discover our sports, music, and clubs.

At Jouline School, we believe in the importance of developing students beyond academics. Our extracurricular activities provide students with opportunities to explore their interests, build teamwork skills, and develop leadership qualities. We offer a variety of engaging activities that cater to different talents and passions.

Here are some of the exciting extracurricular activities available at Jouline School:

  • 1. Sports Teams: Students can join teams for: football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics. We regularly participate in inter-school competitions.
  • 2. Music & Drama Club: Students can express themselves through sSinging, playing instruments, acting, and scriptwriting.
  • 3. Debate & Public Speaking: We have a debate club and public speaking society to help students develop confidence in communication.
  • 4. Science Club: The Science Club fosters a love for STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) through hands-on experiments and research projects.
  • 5. Journalism & Media Club: This club helps students develop skills in writing, photography, and video production, allowing them to create content for the school magazine.
  • 6. Scouting & Community Service: Our students participate in scouting, volunteering, and environmental conservation activities to give back to society.
  • 7. Coding & Robotics Club: We prepare students for the future by teaching coding, robotics, and app development.

These activities not only provide students with fun and enjoyment but also help them develop essential skills such as teamwork, leadership, creativity, and problem-solving. At Jouline School, we encourage every student to participate in at least one extracurricular activity to ensure a well-rounded education.
